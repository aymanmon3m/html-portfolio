Movie Reviews: Top 3 Movies of 2012

A Look Back at the Best Films of the Year.

  1. The Dark Knight Rises:

    2. Nolan's epic conclusion. Intriguing storyline, breathtaking action. Emotionally satisfying farewell to the caped crusader.

  2. Life of Pi:

    3. Visually stunning journey. Captivating tale of survival and self-discovery. A testament to the power of storytelling.

  3. Argo:

    4. Gripping political thriller. Tautly paced, stellar performances. A thrilling ride from start to finish.
